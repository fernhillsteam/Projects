    3D PluraView System – Beam-splitter technology

    How the passive 3D stereo system works

    The 3D PluraView has consistently evolved from Planar’s successful beam-splitter technology, both in function and technology, as well as in design, quality and user ergonomics.

    • Established, mature beam-splitter technology with semi-transparent mirror
    • Passive polarized filter glasses without flicker effect
    • Two professional FHD, 2.5K or 4K monitors
    • Highest component quality with a solid housing and stand – made in Germany
